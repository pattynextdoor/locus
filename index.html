Where do you belong?

Your home isn't just your house

Moving to a new city or neighborhood can be quite daunting.

We get it, you've found your dream home. But how will your morning commute be? How safe is the neighborhood? These are just a few of the factors to consider when moving.

Let Locus help you out. Simply type in an address, and Locus curates points of interest, displays weather trends, scores quality-of-life metrics, and even locates where you could adopt a furry friend.

Powered by state-of-the-art software

Locus uses location-based analytics with maps powered by ArcGIS, and leverages several API's to bring empirical data into your decision-making process. Whether you want to move across school districts or across states, Locus has you covered.
