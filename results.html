How are these scores calculated?

The Teleport API aggregates data from the GeoNames geographical database and computes quality of life scores for cities across various different categories.

The Locus-Curated Community Score leverages over 12 factors from both Teleport's data and Esri Demographics to deliver an aggregate score.
